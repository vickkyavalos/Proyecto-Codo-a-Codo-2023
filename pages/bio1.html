¿Qué es la biología?

Es la ciencia que estudia la vida.The three main types of plant biology are angiosperms, gymnosperms, and ferns. Angiosperms are flowering plants and make up the majority of plant species. They occupy a wide range of niches, from the tops of trees to the bottom of oceans, and contribute to the ecosystem by providing food and shelter for animals, producing oxygen, and absorbing carbon dioxide. Gymnosperms are non-flowering seed plants, such as conifers, and are typically found in colder climates. They contribute to the ecosystem by providing shelter for animals and regulating the water cycle. Ferns are non-seed vascular plants that reproduce through spores and are found in moist environments. They contribute to the ecosystem by preventing soil erosion and providing habitat for animals.
